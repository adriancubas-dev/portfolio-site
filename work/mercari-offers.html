Project

Improving Adoption and Completion of Offers

Mercari — Product Design

Overview

Background

The offers feature on Mercari allowed buyers to negotiate prices with sellers. However, adoption was low and many initiated offers went incomplete. I redesigned the offer flow to increase both the number of offers made and the completion rate of negotiations.

Role

Product Designer

Skills

UX Research, Data Analysis, Interaction Design, A/B Testing

Project Timeline

4 months

← Back to all work
Problem

Low offer adoption and high abandonment

Despite being a key feature for price negotiation, the offers flow had low discoverability and a high abandonment rate. Many users didn't know they could make offers, and those who did often dropped off mid-negotiation.

Research

Identifying friction points

I analyzed funnel data to pinpoint where users dropped off and conducted user interviews to understand mental models around price negotiation. Key findings included confusion about offer etiquette, fear of rejection, and unclear status communication.

Design

Reducing friction and building confidence

I redesigned the offer experience with clearer entry points, guided offer suggestions based on market data, real-time status updates, and encouraging microcopy that reduced anxiety around negotiation.

Key improvements

  • Prominent offer CTA with price suggestion based on similar items
  • Progress indicators showing negotiation status
  • Smart notifications to re-engage both parties
  • Educational tooltips for first-time offer makers
Outcome

Significant increase in completed offers

The redesigned flow led to increased offer initiation and higher completion rates, contributing to more successful transactions and improved buyer-seller satisfaction.
