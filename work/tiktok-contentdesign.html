Project

UX Content Design for Mobile Ad Landing Pages

TikTok — Content Design

Overview

Background

TikTok for Business needed compelling landing page experiences that converted mobile ad viewers into app users and subscribers. I led the content design for multiple ad landing page templates — including app download, carousel, event, and subscription flows.

Role

Content Designer

Skills

UX Writing, Content Strategy, Mobile Design, A/B Testing

Project Timeline

6 months

← Back to all work
Problem

Generic landing pages with low conversion

Existing ad landing pages used boilerplate content that didn't match the energy and tone of the TikTok brand. Conversion rates were below benchmarks, and users often bounced before engaging with the CTA.

Research

Understanding mobile ad behavior

I analyzed user behavior data on existing landing pages, studied competitor approaches, and worked with the ads team to understand advertiser needs. Key insight: users needed immediate value clarity within the first 3 seconds of landing.

Design

Content-first landing page templates

I created content frameworks for four distinct landing page types, each optimized for its conversion goal while maintaining TikTok's playful, direct voice.

App download landing page

Focused on immediate value proposition with social proof and a single clear CTA.

Carousel ad

Outcome

Higher conversion across all templates

The new content-driven templates showed improved conversion rates across all four landing page types. The framework became the standard for TikTok for Business landing page creation.
